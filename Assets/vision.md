Vision:
A short 1-2 hour top down story driven game with puzzles about a human that
accidentally enters a magic world and tries to go back to his world with the
help of his new friends.

What's in scope?
1. 19 explorable areas:
	1. Protagonist home.
	2. Protagonist way from home to magic world entrance.
	3. Way from magic world entrance to magic world village.
	4. Way from magic village to Fire element area.
	5. Fire element area
	6. Fire element dungeon/temple.
	7. Way from magic village to Ice element area.
	8. Ice element area.
	9. Ice element dungeon/temple.
	10. Way from magic village to Earth element area.
	11. Earth element area.
	12. Earth element dungeon/temple.
	13. Way from magic village to Wind element area.
	14. Wind element area.
	15. Wind element temple.
	16. Way from magic village to Ancient altar temple.
	17. Ancient altar entrance.
	18. Ancient altar temple.
	19. Ancient altar room.
2. Day and night light effects when the game story needs it.
3. Dynamic NPC dialogues: A little dialogue when the protagonist makes progress.
4. Main quest.
5. Side quests: Secondary characters will have their own history with side quests.
6. Simple spell casting based combat system.
7. Simple spell leveling system.
8. Increasing enemy ai levels to make enemies in later game history more dificult.

What is not in scope?
1. Comlpex combat mechanics.
2. Complex physics mechanics.
3. Complex speel leveling system.
4. Complex enemy ai system.
